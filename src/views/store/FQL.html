Placing an Order

How do I place an order? +

To place an order, simply browse our collection, select your desired watch, and click "Add to Cart." Proceed to checkout where you'll enter your shipping and payment information. You'll receive an order confirmation email once your purchase is complete.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it? +

We process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer service immediately at service@nomos-glashuette.com or call +212 74889226. We'll do our best to accommodate your request if your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet.

Do you offer gift wrapping? +

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping for all orders. During checkout, you can select the gift wrapping option and include a personalized message. The invoice will be sent separately to your email address.

Payment Options

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfer (for orders within Germany)
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
Is my payment information secure? +

Yes, all payments are processed through secure, encrypted connections. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers. For added security, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and comply with all PCI DSS requirements.

Do you charge sales tax? +

For orders shipped within Germany, we are required by law to charge 19% VAT. For orders shipped to other EU countries, VAT is charged according to local rates. For orders outside the EU, no VAT is charged, but import duties may apply depending on your country's regulations.

Order Confirmation

When will I receive my order confirmation? +

You should receive your order confirmation email immediately after completing your purchase. If you don't see it in your inbox, please check your spam folder. If you still can't find it, please contact our customer service team to verify your order was received.

I didn't receive an order confirmation. What should I do? +

First, please check your spam or junk mail folder. If you still can't find the confirmation email, contact our customer service atmailto:orolux.stor1@gmail.com with your full name and the date/time of your order. We'll verify your order status and resend the confirmation if needed.
